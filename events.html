Special Events and Workshops

The Healing Power of the Breath

With Caroline Pearson

Saturday, November 7, 2015, 2:00-4:30 pm

 

Cost: $35

Based on the teachings of Richard Brown MD, and Patricia Gerbarg, MD, learn simple yet effective breathing techniques which can: increase your stress resilience, renew your energy, increase lung capacity, improve cardio-respiratory health, and improve relationships and empathetic abilities. Breathing exercises will be interspersed with stretching and movement: wear comfortable clothes that you would  use for a yoga class.

Caroline Pearson is a Kripalu certified yoga instructor, and ANCC certified Family Nurse Practitioner. She has been practicing yoga for over 30 years, and teaching both group and private classes since 2011. Caroline led regular weekly yoga classes with students and Club Yoga at St. Lawrence University from 2011-2014.

Caroline holds a particular interest in the healing aspects of yoga for emotional and mental health, including post traumatic stress disorder. She holds certification as a Life Force Yoga teacher for depression and anxiety, and teaches meditation techniques designed by psychiatrists Richard Brown and Patricia Gerbarg, that are simple, but profound in their benefits on health and well being.

 

For more information you can contact her at: athayogini@gmail.com


Yoga and Music to benefit Renewal House

With Trillium staff and friends

Saturday, November 14, 2015, 9:00 am - 7:00 pm

 

Cost: $20 per class

Join us on Saturday, November 14 for a very special day at Trillium Center for Yoga & Health where we will be hosting a fundraiser for the St. Lawrence Valley Renewal House.

We are happy to announce our schedule which includes five yoga classes though out the day, each with live music accompaniment. As well as a midday musical interlude which will be open to the public to drop in, by donation.

The cost is $20.00 per class. Class size will be limited to 15 and will fill up quickly.

To avoid disappointment we suggest that you reserve your mat space in advance.

To do this you can simply see any teacher at Trillium when you are there next or you can email shannon@trilliumyogacenter.com.

You will be asked to pay at the time of space reservation.

We hope that you will take this opportunity to support the St Lawrence Valley Renewal House, support the teachers and musicians who are all generously donating their time and talents, while supporting yourself, body, mind and spirit.

It is going to be fantastic! don't miss it.

Here is what the day looks like:

  • 9:00 am -10:30 am:  Yoga taught by Jean & Shannon. Musical guest Gretchen Koelher
  • 11:00 am -12:30 pm: Yoga taught by Erica Sharpe. Musical guests Allan Cox & Steve Manders
  • 12:45 pm - 1:45 pm: Midday Musical Interlude (Erica Sharpe, Steve Manders, Allan Cox & Dan Gagliardi)
  • 2:00 pm - 3:00 pm: Tabata taught by Tracy Sharlow. Percussion accompaniment
  • 3:30 pm - 5:00 pm: Yoga Taught by Lori. Musical Guest Erica Sharpe
  • 5:30 pm - 7:00 pm: Kundalini Yoga with Gurumantra Kaur. Musical Guest Beth Robinson

All proceeds will go to The St Lawrence Valley Renewal House.
