Former North Country Resident and Grammy Award-Nominee Flutist Peter Phippen in Concert

To benefit the Alzheimer's Association

Friday, October 23 at 7:30 pm

 

Tickets: $12

Grammy Award-nominated flutist and four-time Native American Music Award nominee Peter Phippen returns to Potsdam to perform in concert on Friday, October 23 at 7: 30 p.m.

The concert will be held at the Trillium Center for Yoga & Health at 25 Market Street in downtown Potsdam. A portion of the proceeds from the concert will benefit the Alzheimer’s Association. Tickets are $12 and can be purchased at the door.

Phippen, who grew up in nearby Buckton, is an internationally acclaimed world flutes musician and composer who records and performs music that draws on Native American folk tradition and other folk traditions to create a uniquely ethereal sound. His music has been described as “earthy, improvised and impressionistic, offering listeners a beautifully serene atmosphere for meditation and reflections” (Music Design).

Two of his songs, “Night Song” and “Lullabye,” are currently featured on the digital download compilation, “Native American Flute Music for Meditation” (Canyon Records), that is #48 on iTunes World Music.

“This is a wonderful opportunity to experience Peter Phippens’ transformative music,” said Robin McClellan, who is organizing the concert. “And the meditative space of the Trillium Yoga studio provides the ideal setting.”

For more information, Shannon Miller – shannon@trilliumyogacenter.com or (315) 244 3550

 


The Healing Power of the Breath

With Caroline Pearson

Saturday, November 7, 2015, 2:00-4:30 pm

 

Cost: $35

Based on the teachings of Richard Brown MD, and Patricia Gerbarg, MD, learn simple yet effective breathing techniques which can: increase your stress resilience, renew your energy, increase lung capacity, improve cardio-respiratory health, and improve relationships and empathetic abilities. Breathing exercises will be interspersed with stretching and movement: wear comfortable clothes that you would  use for a yoga class.
