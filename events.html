Mindfulness Immersion Workshop

Charlie Bradt:

Fee: $35, pre-registration preferred, Saturday, September 24, 1 pm – 4 pm, contact shannon@trilliumyogacenter.com

Mindfulness is a process of literally turning our world inside out. We've all been taught to believe that the source of our problems​ is "out there," in the people and events of our lives. Mindfulness says no, the problems are "in here," in the workings of our minds. Which is actually really good news, because if they're "in here," we can do something about them (problems.)

This half-day workshop will give you a chance to look at your world with different eyes, and offer some tools to take with you to begin the process of trading in some stress for a little more peace.​ This workshop would be useful for those just curious as well as those wishing to start a practice.

Charlie Bradt has been teaching and practicing yoga and meditation for over 20 years. In the last four years, he has taught the deeper practice of Mindfulness and Mindful Meditation. Charlie was founder of the Yoga Loft.
