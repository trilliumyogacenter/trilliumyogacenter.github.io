Shine On - Yoga Nidra Workshops with Shannon Miller

Sunday May 15 - 1-2:30, $20

Tuesday May 17- 7-8:30, $20

What is Yoga Nidra?

Can you imagine experiencing deeper levels of health, inner peace of mind, and equanimity?

Modern life leads people to believe that they are relaxing when they drop themselves onto the couch, turn on the TV, reach for a drink, cigarette, coffee, or a good book. These sensory diversions are not the true definition of relaxation. Deep, absolute relaxation is an experience far beyond this. Deep relaxation and inner awareness is healing and transformative on many levels.

Allow me to share with you the practice of Yoga Nidra: Yoga, meaning union or one-pointed awareness, and Nidra, meaning sleep. Often referred to as Divine or Yogic Sleep, this contemporary practice is based on ancient meditation and yogic wisdom. Yoga Nidra is a powerful technique in which you are guided systematically and learn to relax consciously at every level of your being. Yoga Nidra invites you to experience a body scan and breath awareness, as well as a visual and sense component through your physical, energetic, mental, emotional and spiritual layers. With no need to fix or change anything about yourself, you simply notice. In this dawn between sleep and wakefulness you are invited to throw off the burdens, the tensions of life.

During this workshop we will explore more deeply

  • The History of Yoga Nidra
  • A centering meditation
  • What is Yoga Nidra?
  • The five Koshas or bodies of our being
  • Who can practice Yoga Nidra?
  • When and Where?
  • Why - what are the benefits?

Our time together looks something like this:

  • 15-20 minutes of history, context, centering, benefits
  • 15 -20 minutes of very gentle movement
  • 35- 45 minutes of Yoga Nidra
  • 10-15 minutes of integration
